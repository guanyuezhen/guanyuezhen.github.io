Zhenglai Li is a Ph.D. student (supervised by Lizhe Wang) at China University of Geosciences (CUG), China. Previously, he received the BE and MS degree (supervised by Chang Tang) from China University of Geosciences, Wuhan, China, in 2018 and 2021, respectively. His research interests include multi-view clustering, remote sensing change detection.
